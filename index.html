The NCC recognizes the city of Monterrey for the coordinated work to reduce crime towards local business

The president of the NCC (National Chamber of Commerce), services and tourism, presented the report of security in relation with the business sector in the city of Monterrey, which states that during the year 2022, the number of local businesses that were victims of at least one felony dropped from 21% to 15.8% comparated to the last year 2021. The governor of the state, said that in the last 10 years there had not been such a significant decrease in that category, and there was also an increment in the complaints for robbery and assault.
